About Gadzeke

Gadzeke is a home for the words that stuck. Every quote here was pulled from a book actually read — underlined because it changed how something looked, not generated by AI or scraped from a list. Just the lines worth remembering, gathered one book at a time.

Have a quote from your own reading that deserves a place here? Send it our way at ramizeke516@gmail.com — we'd love to see what's stuck with you.

Foundation

Gadzeke was created by Rami Zeke, who believes the best idea in a book is often just a sentence or two — and that a sentence worth underlining is worth sharing with someone who might need to read it today. This site is a running collection of those sentences, for anyone who loves books enough to want the best parts within easy reach.
